About me

I am a Sêr Cymru Research Fellow at Cardiff University.

My research focuses on improving analysis of data from ground-based laser interferometer gravitational-wave observatories (e.g. LIGO and Virgo) by developing better software.

From my perspective 'better' is synonymous with 'easier to use', meaning the software should be:
  • intuitive (easy to use without reading the manual),
  • well-documented (has a good manual), and
  • robust (doesn't crash when it shouldn't)
